About Me

Background

I’m excited to grow in web development because it blends creativity with purpose, I want to design websites that are not only visually appealing but also easy to use, accessible, and meaningful. My goal is to create online experiences that reflect the needs of real people, especially those whose voices are often overlooked. This journey is personal to me, and I’m ready to keep learning, building, and making a difference through thoughtful design and development.
